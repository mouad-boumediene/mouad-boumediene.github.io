Projects

HM-DRL Experiment

A heatmap-based heuristic to guide multi-agent pathfinding with distributed Deep Reinforcement Learning. Improves success rate and traffic flows in crowded grid-based environments.

Traffic Aware MAPF

A DRL-based multi-robot planning approach that adapts paths in real-time based on congestion levels and local "traffic jams," aiming to reduce overall travel time for the fleet.

Scalability Experiment on den520d Map

A large-scale MAPF experiment run on the den520d map (often used in pathfinding research). Tested how system scales with increasing number of agents, measuring success rate and average path length.

4D Trajectory Planning

Contributed to the Gen4jectory algorithm for multi-UAV flight with minimized flight times. Involves a 4D approach (x, y, z + time) ensuring collision-free flight paths.
